What is the physical web?

The Physical Web enables individuals quick and interactions with physical objects and locations because all the individual has to do is walk up to a location where the physical web can be used to interact with an object which could be a parking meter, a toy, a poster or even something else. In terms of location this could be a bus stop, a museum, a store or maybe something else.

How does the physical web work?

The way teh physical web works is that there is no need in worrying about the memory of your device because the physical web does not require you to install an app first on your device to view it.
